About InternMatch

InternMatch is an AI-powered recommendation platform designed to match students with the most suitable internship opportunities under the PM Internship Scheme and beyond. Our mission is to bridge the gap between students and opportunities by analyzing skills, interests, and career aspirations to deliver personalized matches.

Unlike traditional portals, InternMatch enhances discovery using data-driven insights, helping students not just find internships but also build career pathways with confidence.
